# Hi, I'm Swapnil.

I write about artificial intelligence and machine learning — how the models actually work, how the systems behind them are built, and what it all means for the people building with them.

No hype. No surface-level takes. Just clear explanations of things worth understanding.

## What you'll find here

Posts on this blog fall into a few categories:

- **How things work** — transformer architectures, attention mechanisms, embeddings, inference pipelines. The stuff that's often explained poorly elsewhere.
- **Building with AI** — practical guides on using ML tools, APIs, and open-source models to build real things.
- **What's actually happening** — honest takes on AI trends, research papers worth reading, and industry moves that matter.

If you've ever read a breathless AI article and thought *"but how does it actually work?"* — this is the blog for you.

## Why I write this

I got tired of AI content that either oversimplifies to the point of uselessness or hides behind jargon to sound smart. There's a middle ground — technically honest but still readable — and that's what I'm trying to build here.

I also build things. Most posts come from something I was actually trying to understand or implement, which means the questions I'm answering are real ones.
